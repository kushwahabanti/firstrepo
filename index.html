Rohtas Fort

Rohtas Fort is a majestic historical site located in the Kaimur hills. Built during the reign of Sher Shah Suri, it stands as a symbol of architectural brilliance and cultural heritage. Explore the fort's vast courtyards, intricate carvings, and breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes.
