Rohtas Fort

Overview

Rohtas Fort, also known as Rohtasgarh Fort, is one of the most significant historical monuments in Bihar. Built by Sher Shah Suri in the 16th century, the fort stands on the Kaimur plateau and is surrounded by lush forests and dramatic landscapes. It attracts tourists, history lovers, photographers, and adventure seekers alike.
